About Me

Hi! I'm Parand Khalili, an undergraduate computer engineering student at Amirkabir University of Technology with a deep interest in data, systems, and software design.

Currently, I'm exploring the world of data analysis and machine learning. I'm taking online courses and practical projects to improve my skills and move toward my goal of becoming a data analyst. I'm also passionate about understanding how systems work—from operating systems and databases to scalable backend designs.

Aside from tech, I truly enjoy music, reading books, and learning new things across different domains. Curiosity has always driven me—whether it’s debugging a piece of code or diving into a completely new field.

I’ve had the chance to work on several meaningful projects, from building backend services in Django to simulating games in Verilog, and I’m currently serving as a Teaching Assistant in both technical and theoretical courses such as Advanced Programming, Applied Linear Algebra, and Software Engineering.
